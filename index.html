Got questions?

How does WAVE work?
Connect your X timeline, your podcast subscriptions, and the news sources you trust. Pick the duration you want to listen for. Our AI selects the most relevant moments from each source, orders them for narrative flow, and synthesizes a host voice — so what would have been hours of scrolling becomes one focused audio show.
What sources can I connect?
X / Twitter timelines, public podcast RSS feeds, Apple Podcasts and Spotify subscriptions, RSS news feeds (NYT, Reuters, FT, your favorite Substacks), YouTube channels, and Instagram saved reels. More sources are added every week — tell us what's missing.
Which languages are supported?
English and Spanish at launch, with French, German, Portuguese, and Japanese coming over the next few weeks. The AI host can read sources in one language and host the show in another — useful if your news is in English but you'd rather listen in Spanish.
Can I download episodes?
Yes — every episode is available for offline listening on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and as a downloadable MP3. Built for the commute, the walk, the workout, the flight.
Is WAVE free to use?
The first 22 minutes a day are free, forever. Pro tier unlocks unlimited duration, custom host voices, multi-language hosting, and the ability to turn any source into a podcast. We're building in public — see the manifesto and follow the build log.
I have more questions!
DM us on X, file an issue on the public repo, or hit reply on the morning episode. We answer every one — the team is small and we read every word.
